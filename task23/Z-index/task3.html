APPLE

Research shows the antioxidants in apples can slow the growth of cancer cells. And they can protect the cells in your pancreas, which can lower your chances of type 2 diabetes. Scientists also give apples credit for helping: Your lung strength.

PIZZA

About 350 slices of pizza are consumed every second in the US. 93% of Americans order a pizza at least once a month. Pepperoni is America's favourite pizza topping. Pizza consumption is highest during Big Game Days.

TEA

Tea is the most popular beverage in the world after water. It's a simple preparation of pouring hot water over cured leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant.The first recording of tea described it as a medicinal beverage in China in the 3rd century AD.
